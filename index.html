Dino Dash

Goal

Help Danny, the energetic green dinosaur, run fast across the land and jumping over random lava holes!

Instructions

  1. Choose your typing exercise by selecting "Demo text", or "Custom Text" with your custom text of at least 100 characters. A word will be randomly selected in either choice.
  2. Start the game by pressing the 'START' button below.
  3. To jump over a hole, type the word in the box under the prompted word. Hit the 'enter' key to clear the jump. Prompted word will turn green if successful.
  4. The game ends when there are three strikes (indication of strikes in top right corner of screen). You have the chance to type the same word for second try. More on strike policy below in the next section.
  5. After finishing the game, you may wish to enter your name to be on the scorelist! The scoring is based on the number of correct words.
  6. When the game is over, you can click on the 'reset' button to reset the game or the 'restart' button to play again.
  7. Have fun!

Reasoning for strike

  1. Failure to type the prompted word.
  2. Entered the correct word in too early.
  3. Entered an incorrect word.
  4. Red font color will tell you that you entered the word incorrectly.
