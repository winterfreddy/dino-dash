Dino Dash

Goal

Help Danny, the energetic green dinosaur, run fast across the land and jumping over random lava holes!

Please read the in-game instructions throughly first!

Pre-game Instructions

  1. Choose your typing exercise by selecting "Demo text", or "Custom Text" with your custom text of at least 100 characters. A word will be randomly selected in either choice.
  2. Start the game by pressing the 'START' button below. The text box on game screen will be active so you can type right away!

In-game Instructions

  1. The prompted word will appear on the screen! Type that word in the textbox just below it.
  2. After entering the word, hit the 'enter' key when the dino is near the hole.
  3. This allows the dinosaur to jump successfully. If successful, the prompted word will turn green. Otherwise, it can either be black or red.
  4. If the prompted word is red, that means one strike (indicated in top right corner of screen). Otherwise, a strike is imposed and you still have the chance to type the same word for another try.
  5. If you messed up during the game, you can click on the 'RESET' button to start over again.
  6. HAVE FUN!

End-game notes:

  1. The game ends when there are three strikes (or three missed words). More on strike policy below in the next section.
  2. After finishing the game, you may wish to enter your name to be on the scorelist! The scoring is based on the number of correct words.
  3. When the game is over, you can click on the 'RESET' button to play again. Don't forget to hit the 'START' button!

Reasoning for strike

  1. Failure to type the prompted word.
  2. Entered the correct word in too early.
  3. Entered an incorrect word.
