Dino Dash

Goal

The goal is to have the dinosaur run across the land, skipping over holes, as you complete your typing exercise.

Instructions

  1. Choose your typing exercise by selecting "Demo text" for randomly generated words or input your custom text and select "Custom Text". Text minimum is at least 100 characters.
  2. Start the game by pressing the 'START' button below.
  3. To jump over a hole, type the word on screen successfully in the text box below the game screen. Hit 'enter' key successively.
  4. The game ends when there are three strikes (you trip over a hole three times) or you finished the typing exercise.
  5. After finishing the game and if you made it to being in the top ten, enter your name to be entered!
  6. When the game is over, you can click on the 'reset' button to reset the game.
  7. Have fun!
